Important Threats

Flood

What is it? A sudden rise of water that can destroy homes and roads.

Prevention: Avoid building near rivers or valleys. Keep important items upstairs. Always have an emergency bag ready.

During Flood: Move to higher ground quickly. Avoid bridges or flooded streets. Turn off electricity.

Dust Storm / Strong Wind

What is it? Very strong winds often carrying dust or rain.

Prevention: Close doors and windows tightly. Bring outdoor objects (pots, tables) inside.

During the Event: Take shelter indoors. Stay away from windows.

Gas Explosion

What is it? Gas leaking and exploding in kitchens or other areas.

Prevention: Always turn off the gas valve after use. Take gas smells seriously. Check pipes yearly.

During Explosion: Do not use lighters or electrical switches. Leave immediately and call fire department.

Power Outage / Energy Crisis

What is it? Long blackout especially at night or winter.

Prevention: Keep flashlights and spare batteries ready. Always charge your mobile. Have blankets and warm clothes.

Health Issues / Epidemics

What is it? Diseases that spread quickly (like COVID-19 or cholera).

Prevention: Wash hands, wear masks, keep distance from others. Avoid contaminated food and water.

Missing Person / Vulnerable People

Prevention: Always keep contact info in child’s bag or clothes. Teach them to seek police or trusted adults.

Quick Survival Tips

Keep Calm and Think Clearly

In emergencies, staying calm helps you make better decisions.

Always Have a Charged Phone

Keep your mobile fully charged and carry a power bank.

Prepare an Emergency Bag

Include water, flashlight, first aid, food, and important documents.

Know Your Exits

Learn emergency exits and evacuation routes wherever you are.

Listen to Official Instructions

Follow local authority announcements carefully.

Survival Kit Essentials

Always keep these items ready for emergencies:

  • 💧 Water bottles (at least 3 days supply)
  • 🔦 Flashlight with extra batteries
  • 🍫 Non-perishable food items
  • 🩹 First aid kit
  • 📱 Fully charged mobile phone & power bank
  • 🧥 Warm clothes and blankets
  • 📜 Important documents in waterproof bag
  • 🛠️ Multi-tool or pocket knife
