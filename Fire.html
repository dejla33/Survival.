Before a Fire

Plan Escape Routes

Make sure every family member knows two ways out of each room. Practice drills regularly.

Install Smoke Alarms

Put alarms on every level and test them monthly. Change batteries twice a year.

Check Fire Extinguishers

Keep extinguishers handy, especially in the kitchen. Learn how to use them correctly.

During a Fire

Stay Low

Smoke rises. Stay close to the ground and crawl under smoke to get out safely.

Check Doors

If the door is hot, do not open it. Use another exit. If trapped, call for help from a window.

Don‚Äôt Re-enter

Once out, stay out. Never return into a burning building for anything.

After a Fire

Contact Authorities

Call emergency services. Do not enter the building until cleared by professionals.

Seek Support

Find help for trauma, insurance claims, and temporary shelter if needed.

Rebuild Safely

Only begin repairs after the area is fully safe and documented by insurance.
