Airstrikes are sudden and extremely dangerous. Here's a complete guide to staying safe:

1. Seek Shelter Immediately: Go to the basement or the most secure room with no windows. Avoid open spaces.
2. Lie Low: Lay flat on the ground to avoid injuries from flying debris. Protect your head and neck with your arms.
3. Stay Away from Windows: Glass can shatter and cause injuries. Stay behind thick walls or under furniture if needed.
4. Turn Off Electronics: Phones and radios may attract signals. Use battery-powered devices for emergency broadcasts.
5. After the Airstrike: Wait for an all-clear signal. Avoid touching unknown objects. They might be unexploded ordnance.
